A Boutique TriBeCa Hotel

Cosmopolitan Hotel - TriBeCa

Located in the exciting neighborhood of TriBeCa, The Cosmopolitan offers exceptional service, upscale amenities and a prime location at our TriBeCa hotel.

Historic TriBeCa and neighboring Soho are at the epicenter of the New York City art, shopping and restaurant scene. The Cosmopolitan Hotel in TriBeCa NYC is located a few blocks away from the 9/11 Memorial, the new World Trade Center and City Hall. Fashionable Greenwich Village is within walking distance as well as Wall Street and the Financial District. In addition to providing a firsthand experience of the unique New York City downtown culture, this TriBeCa hotel offers easy access to all New York City attractions with the Chambers Street Subway station conveniently located just steps from the hotel.

The hotel’s personable staff ensures guests have a memorable experience while visiting New York City. The Cosmopolitan Hotel –TriBeCa offers an array of upscale amenities, such as Frette linens, CO Bigelow bath products, Room Service by Kitchenette, complimentary Wi-Fi and iPod docks. Guests can also enjoy the hotel’s state of the art Fitness room as well as a full service Starbucks on hotel premises.
