An Artful NoMad Hotel

The Evelyn

Quirky, fun, and unpretentious, The Evelyn is a relaxed and friendly hotel in Manhattan’s NoMad neighborhood. The Evelyn stands alone among NoMad hotels, carrying more than a century of history within its walls and a hearty dose of artistic character.

The Evelyn features many upscale amenities, including fitness club access, concierge service, discounted parking, and Internet kiosks. Each room also includes complimentary Wi-Fi, cable TV and a selection of bath amenities.

This NoMad hotel is the ideal destination for those seeking the preeminent Manhattan experience. Union Square, Madison Square Park, Fifth Avenue and Broadway are all within walking distance, along with some of Manhattan’s best shopping, dining and nightlife venues.
