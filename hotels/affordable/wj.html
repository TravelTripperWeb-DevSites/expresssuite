An Unpretentious Theater District Hotel

Washington Jefferson Hotel

Set on-a tranquil, tree-lined block just steps away from the bright lights of Broadway, the Washington Jefferson Hotel provides a comfortable and relaxing stay for leisure and business travelers alike, set in the heart of Manhattan’s brightest neighborhood, The Theater District.

This boutique Theater District hotel features 158 rooms and suites, each appointed with free Wi-Fi access, Frette linens and towels, iPod docking stations, luxurious bathroom amenities and accommodating layouts. The hotel puts guest satisfaction first, employing a team of friendly and knowledgeable hospitality professionals who know the city inside and out.

A wide variety of essential NYC attractions and experiences are within easy reach of this Theater District hotel near Times Square. Broadway theaters are just steps from the hotel’s front door, while Central Park, Rockefeller Center, and Fifth Avenue are also within convenient walking distance.
