A Historic Upper West Side Hotel

Hotel Belleclaire

Classic charm and modern conveniences come together at Hotel Belleclaire, a historic Upper West Side hotel. Housed in a 1903 Emery Roth landmark building, the hotel offers a wide range of sophisticated amenities, both in-room and onsite.

Each of the 240 accommodations at our hotel in NYC provides a haven for rest and relaxation. Rooms and suites include complimentary Wi-Fi access, C.O. Bigelow bath amenities, luxurious Frette linens, flat-screen HD TVs and sleek wood laminate floors. This affordable boutique hotel on the Upper West Side also offers guests perks like a well-equipped media lounge, arrival refreshment, overnight shoeshine, and use of a 24-hour fitness center.

Hotel Belleclaire is located on a tree-lined street – just steps from Broadway – within walking distance of Central Park.
