A Small Luxury NYC Hotel

The Iroquois New York

A 4-star small luxury NYC hotel tucked away on a quiet block in the heart of Midtown Manhattan, the Iroquois New York combines classic design and contemporary amenities to create a quintessentially New York City experience.

The Iroquois New York offers 114 elegantly appointed guest rooms and nine spacious suites, each featuring Comfy Simmons Beautyrest mattresses, comfortable work stations and Jacuzzi tubs. The Midtown NYC hotel’s onsite amenities include a 24-hour fitness center, Finnish sauna, complimentary services are Wi-Fi access, coffee and overnight shoeshine.

The Iroquois is more than just an exceptional Midtown Manhattan hotel. The well-located property is also home to Triomphe Restaurant & Lounge, Midtown's best French restaurant, and Lantern’s Keep, a prohibition-era-inspired cocktail salon. Stay at The Iroquois to experience one of the best luxury hotels NYC has to offer.
