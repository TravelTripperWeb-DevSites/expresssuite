Hotel Chandler

Discover the quintessential New York City experience of the Hotel Chandler, a smart choice among Flatiron NYC hotels. Located in the vibrant Flatiron District neighborhood, this Manhattan boutique hotel combines a rich architectural history with a forward-thinking style that includes modern amenities and contemporary design details.

Set in a 1905 Beaux-Arts building just steps from Fifth Avenue, Hotel Chandler conveys an air of classic, stately elegance while featuring the latest in modern amenities. Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, a modern fitness center, in-room dining and dedicated concierge staff make Hotel Chandler an ideal destination for business or leisure travel.

Hotel Chandler hosts a restaurant as well as the Hotel Chandler Bar. The Hotel Chandler restaurant offers an array of unique culinary creations emphasizing fresh ingredients and seasonal inspiration. Come experience this boutique hotel in the Flatiron District.
