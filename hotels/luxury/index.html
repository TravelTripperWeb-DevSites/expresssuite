New York Luxury Hotels

Stay in Upscale, Boutique Style

New York City, known as the finance and media capital of the world, carries an unmistakable air of refinement that is infused into the upscale accommodations at Triumph Hotels’ pair of small luxury hotels – the Iroquois New York and Hotel Chandler. Stay with the best luxury hotels NYC has to offer.

Each of these distinct Midtown Manhattan hotels goes above and beyond to offer a quintessential Manhattan experience. Well-appointed accommodations include luxury bedding, Frette linens and Molton Brown bath amenities. Meeting spaces include AV equipment and personal service. Featuring high-end dining at Hotel Chandler Restaurant and Triomphe and carefully curated cocktails at Lantern’s Keep and Hotel Chandler Bar, these luxury NYC hotels achieve a high level of sophistication while maintaining a welcoming and personal atmosphere.
