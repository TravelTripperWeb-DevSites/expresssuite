Brilliant Theater District Hotels

Washington Jefferson Hotel

The bright lights, bustling sidewalks and shimmering skyscrapers for which New York City is known are concentrated in Midtown Manhattan, most notably in the area surrounding Times Square. This neighborhood – known as the Theater District – is home to some of the city’s top attractions, including Broadway theaters, music venues, corporate offices and shopping destinations. Staying at one of New York's Theater District hotels grants you enviable access to some of the best sights in the city.

New York City has revolved around the Theater District and Midtown for decades, but the area has only recently undergone a transformation into a fully-realized tourist destination. Popular retailers line Broadway as it stretches through Times Square, world-famous theaters dot the side streets, and major corporate offices populate the skyscrapers towering over the area.

The Washington Jefferson Hotel in Theater District NYC provides the key to a memorable experience in Midtown Manhattan. As a conveniently located Times Square boutique hotel – just several blocks from the heart of the Theater District – the WJ showcases architectural details that recall New York City’s prewar Gilded era.
