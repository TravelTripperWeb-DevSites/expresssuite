Lively NoMad Hotels

The Evelyn

The NoMad, or North of Madison Square Park, frames the quaint little park near the very heart of the city. This diverse neighborhood with a wealth of culinary, nightlife and shopping options, as well as distinct NoMad hotels, offers something for everyone.

The area merges seamlessly with the borders of Midtown, with corporate offices and tourist attractions just a short subway or taxi ride away. Upscale restaurants and busy bars line the neighborhood’s avenues, while the cross-streets are quiet, tree-lined and stately, graced with elegant brownstones, larger apartment buildings and charming hotels in NoMad.

The Evelyn Hotel stands out as a signature example of the marriage of history and modernity in the neighborhood. This hotel in NoMad – just one block north of Madison Square Park – blends traditional architectural details with playful light fixtures to create a marquee that is exhilarating and memorable.
