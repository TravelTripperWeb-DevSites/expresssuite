Times Square

Hotel Edison

Times Square is the source of New York City's reputation as the City That Never Sleeps. Presided over by the world's major media companies, Times Square pulsates with power and energy. The bright lights of the billboards, busy entertainment venues and major events - including New Year's Eve - make Times Square in Manhattan one of the most immediately recognizable tourist attractions in NYC and the world. With fast access to theaters, shops, stores and offices, it's an unforgettable place to discover the city. 

Hotel Edison in Times Square NYC provides the key to a memorable vacation in Manhattan. As a conveniently located Times Square hotel – just several blocks from the heart of the Theater District – the Edison Hotel is a classic example of Art Deco magnificence and fine service.
