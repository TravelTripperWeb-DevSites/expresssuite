Midtown Manhattan is the figurative heart and literal commercial engine of New York City, serving millions of commuters, tourists and residents daily. At street level, cafés, clothing retailers and restaurants make for a lively scene. Overhead, shimmering skyscrapers are populated by corporate offices, hotels and high-end residences.
