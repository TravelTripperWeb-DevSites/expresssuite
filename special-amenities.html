IT FEELS GOOD TO BE A GUEST AT TRIUMPH HOTELS

IN PARTNERSHIP WITH FITRESERVE, ENJOY ACCESS TO OVER 400 NYC FITNESS STUDIOS

Triumph Hotels and FitReserve, premier fitness membership offering access to the top boutique fitness studios in New York City, have joined forces to debut a brand-new FlexPack option created specially for guests of Triumph Hotels. The TravelPack* offers business and leisure guests alike access to three fitness classes during their visit to NYC with over 44,000 classes and 400 studios to choose from for the exclusive price of $54.00. Whether guests prefer yoga, Pilates, barre, spinning, rowing, CrossFit, boot camp or dance, there are fun fitness options for everyone at some of NYC’s most buzzed about studios including Peloton, CITYROW, Exceed, Yoga Vida, Uptown Pilates, Aerospace High Performance Center, The Bar Method and many more. A one-of-a-kind limited membership, the TravelPack remains active for three months only, so Triumph guests can enjoy their workouts while in town without having to worry about any recurring payments or extended membership commitments.

*Does not include all studios. Barry’s Bootcamp and Tennis in Manhattan (Vanderbilt Tennis Club, Sutton East Tennis Club and Yorkville Tennis Club) only available on FitReserve Gold and Platinum plans. Guests may only attend each studio one time per month on the TravelPack plan.
