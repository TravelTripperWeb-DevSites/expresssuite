Unlock the New York City Experience with Triumph Hotels

The Key to New York City

From the stately pre-war architecture of the Flatiron District to the bright lights of Broadway to the tree-lined side streets of the Upper West Side, no city offers the diversity of experience like New York.

It is the city that never sleeps, the finance and media capital of the world, steward of 29,000 acres of parkland, and home to more than 8 million people. It is this diversity and character that you will find reflected in each of Triumph’s seven New York City hotels.

Architecture – Each of Triumph’s hotels resides in a distinguished and unique building, offering a rich cross-section of New York City’s unparalleled architectural history.

Neighborhood – New York is a city of distinct neighborhoods, each with its own signature style and character. Triumph emerges their guests into the heart of seven of the city’s most incomparable areas.

Service – Triumph’s classic and gracious hospitality extends past the walls of our NYC hotels. Our expert staff is your key to the city, guiding you toward some of the best under-the-radar dining destinations and hard-to-get event tickets.

History – With a history stretching back three generations, Triumph Hotels’ connection with New York City includes James Dean, Abraham Lincoln and many other luminaries who have stayed at our hotels.

Design – Residential, comfortable and unpretentiously elegant, each Triumph accommodation is graced with distinctive design inspired by its immediate neighborhood and the character of the city as a whole.

At Triumph, we offer a diverse selection of top New York hotels.
