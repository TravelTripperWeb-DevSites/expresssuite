Historic Hotels in New York City

Authentic Architecture

Each of our historic hotels in New York is an architectural gem – a reflection of the city, neighborhood, and era in which it was designed and built. Triumph's collection of hotels delivers a unique architectural experience and common to all are a set of attributes that make every stay distinctive and memorable.

Authentic – Each hotel holds a certain heritage and a deep-rooted narrative, from the historic industrial style of Cosmopolitan Hotel - TriBeCa to the Beaux Arts splendor of Hotel Chandler to the early-20th century elegance of Hotel Belleclaire.

Distinct – Eclectic, diverse and inviting, each of Triumph’s historic hotels in NYC feature distinctive room compositions and common spaces that carry the past into the present, offering a tangible sense of place, and providing memorable experiences.

Iconic – NYC architecture is about iconic styles and unmistakable symbols, and the Triumph collection beautifully reflects the city’s architectural range with an array of breathtaking elements and signature details.
