Travel Agent Rewards in New York City

Book More, Earn More

Triumph Hotels is happy to reward the hard work of travel professionals with our travel agent rewards program. Our program provides a $100 American Express gift card for every 10 bookings consumed to travel agents who book at any Triumph Hotel over the course of one quarter. Just book the room using a valid Travel Agent ID and submit your confirmation numbers via email to agents@triumphny.com.

Before you can qualify, you must sign up below to earn your rewards. 

Terms and Conditions:

  • Fill out the below RFP form to become part of our program.
  • Book 10 room nights at any one of our 7 Triumph Hotels during a quarter.
  • Please submit your confirmation numbers to agents@triumphny.com by the 7th of the 1st month of each quarter.
  • If you are a Rewards member and have submitted your confirmation numbers, you will then qualify to receive your gift card by the 21st of the 1st month of the following quarter.
  • For group inquiries, please contact the hotel directly.
