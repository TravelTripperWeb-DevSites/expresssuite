Triumph Hotels

Travel Agent Rewards Program

Triumph Hotels helps guests unlock unique New York City experiences.

With seven distinct hotels in some of Manhattan's best neighborhoods, Triumph celebrates the diversity and depth of the surrounding city with exceptional service, inviting amenities, and contemporary design. Our collection of hotels is inspired by New York City -- a feeling we strive to pass along to each and every guest. When your clients experience NYC at Triumph Hotels, they'll enjoy:

  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Free expert-led walking tours of signature NYC neighborhoods
  • Access to unique New York dining experiences at Triomphe, Lantern's Keep, and more
  • Comfortable accommodations in the heart of one of the city's most engaging neighborhoods
  • Friendly NYC know-how from local expert concierge and front desk staff
  • Ticketing assistance for Broadway shows, sporting events, and live music

Triumph Hotels works actively with travel agents to provide clients with unforgettable New York City experiences. We acknowledge travel professionals for their hard work by providing a lucrative travel agent rewards program and access to our best deals and promotions. The Triumph Travel Agent Rewards program is our way of saying thank you!

Triumph Travel Agent Rewards - How It Works:

  • Make all of your bookings using a valid Travel Agent ID number
  • When you reach 10 bookings by your clients within one quarter, complete this form
  • For every 10 consumed bookings, we will provide a $100 American Express gift card to you as a token of our appreciation
  • Be sure to submit your form by the 7th of the first month of each quarter in order to receive your reward by the 21st of the first month of the following quarter
