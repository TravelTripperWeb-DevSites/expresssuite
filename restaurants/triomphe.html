A Standout Among Midtown NYC Restaurants

Triomphe Restaurant at The Iroquois New York

Fresh flavors and subtle sophistication combined with a tranquil setting make Triomphe Restaurant & Lounge, one of Midtown's most critically acclaimed and locally beloved restaurants. The subject of rave reviews in The New York Times, New York Observer and Crain’s New York Business, Triomphe has stood as one of New York’s premier French restaurants since opening in 2000.

From lighter options like fresh fruit and steel cut oatmeal in the morning to midday favorites like smoked salmon and grilled striped bass to dinner entrees such as ricotta cavatelli and five-spiced muscovy duck breast, Triomphe serves a diverse selection of dishes.
